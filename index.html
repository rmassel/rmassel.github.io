About Rick Massel

Rick Massel (Richard Massel) operates at the intersection of human systems and digital change. A former New York City High School Principal turned SaaS operator, Rick has spent two decades bridging the gap between institutional reality and high-growth technology.

He believes the best way to understand the future of AI is to analyze the worlds we have already imagined. Through his writing in Data Driven Investor and Towards AI, he uses fiction to teach the "mechanics" of thinking, helping leaders navigate the complex transition from legacy systems to a generative future.

His perspective is shaped by a career that spans continents—from leading educational initiatives in India, China, and Angola to managing Customer Success strategy in the US. When he isn't decoding the latest AI models, he is an endurance athlete who has trekked the Himalayas and visited over 60 countries.
